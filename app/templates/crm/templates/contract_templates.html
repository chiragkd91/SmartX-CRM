SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of [Date]

1. PARTIES

This agreement is entered into between [Company Name], a [State] corporation with its principal place of business at [Address] ("Provider"), and [Client Name], a [State] corporation with its principal place of business at [Address] ("Client").

2. SERVICES

The Provider shall provide the following services to the Client: [Service Description]. The Provider shall perform all services in a professional manner and in accordance with industry standards.

3. PAYMENT TERMS

The Client shall pay the Provider [Amount] for the services rendered. Payment shall be made within [Payment Terms] days of receipt of invoice. Late payments shall incur interest at the rate of [Interest Rate]% per month.

4. TERM AND TERMINATION

This agreement shall commence on [Start Date] and continue until [End Date], unless earlier terminated as provided herein. Either party may terminate this agreement upon [Notice Period] days written notice to the other party.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary information disclosed during the term of this agreement. This obligation shall survive the termination of this agreement for a period of [Confidentiality Period] years.
